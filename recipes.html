Chocolate Cake
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Cocoa Powder 1/2 cup
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 2
Instructions for Making Chocolate Cake
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of flour, 1 cup of sugar, and 1/2 cup of cocoa powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat 2 eggs.
  4. Add the beaten eggs and 1 cup of milk to the dry mixture.
  5. Mix the ingredients thoroughly until smooth.
  6. Grease a cake pan and pour the batter into it.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Remove the cake from the oven and allow it to cool.
  9. Once cool, frost the cake with your choice of icing.
  10. Enjoy your homemade chocolate cake!
